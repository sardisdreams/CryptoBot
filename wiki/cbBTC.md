# Coinbase Wrapped Bitcoin (cbBTC)

## What it is
cbBTC is Bitcoin wrapped by Coinbase, redeemable 1:1 for BTC. It brings Bitcoin liquidity onto Base and Ethereum so it can be used in DeFi without leaving the chain. Backed by actual BTC held in Coinbase custody.

## Fundamentals
- 1:1 backed by BTC held in Coinbase custody (centralized trust assumption)
- Bitcoin supply: 21M hard cap — ~19.7M mined as of 2024
- Bitcoin halving occurs every ~4 years (next ~2028)
- Bitcoin dominance historically rises in early bull markets
- cbBTC vs WBTC: cbBTC is newer, Coinbase-backed; WBTC is older, BitGo-backed

## Trading notes
- Typically less volatile than ETH on a % basis in bear markets
- Leads ETH during institutional buying phases
- cbBTC/USDC pool on Aerodrome — check liquidity before large trades
- Thin liquidity compared to WETH/USDC — use smaller position sizes or expect higher slippage

## Risk factors
- Counterparty risk: Coinbase holds the BTC (centralized)
- Liquidity on Base thinner than on Ethereum mainnet
- Regulatory risk around Coinbase specifically
