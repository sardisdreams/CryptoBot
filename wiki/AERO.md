# Aerodrome Finance (AERO)

## What it is
Aerodrome is Base's primary decentralized exchange and liquidity hub. It uses a vote-lock governance model (veAERO) and incentivizes liquidity with emissions. It is the Velodrome fork on Base — the dominant AMM by TVL.

## Fundamentals
- Base's largest DEX by TVL — the bot uses Aerodrome for its own trade execution
- ~$350M market cap (as of June 2026)
- $20M+ daily volume — real, deep liquidity
- Revenue-generating protocol: swap fees accrue to veAERO holders
- Audited by Ether Authority (June 2024)
- 744,000+ token holders — well distributed

## Trading notes
- Good Setup 2 (Dip Recovery) candidate: dips with broader market but recovers
- When BTC recovers, AERO tends to recover faster than average (DeFi beta)
- veAERO locking reduces circulating supply — limits sell pressure
- Watch for TVL growth as a leading indicator of AERO price strength

## Key catalysts to watch
- New protocol integrations (more volume = more fees = more AERO demand)
- Base ecosystem growth driving DEX usage
- veAERO epoch votes — high bribe activity signals strong demand

## Risk factors
- AERO emissions are ongoing — continuous sell pressure from yield farmers
- Governance token risk: if a better DEX launches on Base, TVL could migrate
- Correlated with ETH and broader DeFi sentiment
